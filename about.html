About Me

I'm Nathaniel Bubune, a passionate Computer Science student with a love for solving real-world problems using technology. My journey in software development is driven by curiosity, creativity, and a hunger to continuously improve.

I’m a certified Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS), a versatile Full-Stack Developer, and a dedicated Database Administrator. I write clean, efficient Object-Oriented Programs and build scalable backend services, intuitive user interfaces, and responsive applications that users love.

On top of that, I’m an almost pro-level Graphic Designer and a budding expert in UI/UX Design. I take pride in transforming complex ideas into elegant digital solutions.

When I'm not coding, you’ll find me learning new skills, refining my craft, or creating content to share what I’ve learned with the world. I thrive in collaborative environments where ideas turn into impactful solutions.
